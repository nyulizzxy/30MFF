ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Liz. I'm Vietnamese-American, born and raised in New York City. I'm interested in content creation, jewelry-making, and spending all day on roller coasters! Being a Native New Yorker didn't mean much to me before university, rather, being Vietnamese was the identity I was more proud over. It wasn't until I came to NYU in Abu Dhabi that I started feeling differently about my New Yorker identity.
